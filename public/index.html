Welcome to BBall Data-Viz

Have you ever wondered how much money your favorite basketball player makes per minute? We have the answers for you.

Money per minute shows the amount of money each player is making per minute they are on the court. I assumed all players are going to play 82 games this year to find their salary per games played, then I took their avg minutes per game to find the salary per minute of play time.

You can see the data for the entire NBA or filter it to your favorite team. Compare players by position to get an idea of their relative value.

We will continue to add more graphs in the future so remember to check back often.
